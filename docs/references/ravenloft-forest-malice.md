# Ravenloft Forest Malice Features

Generic malice abilities for monsters in a scary forest setting. Based on patterns from published Draw Steel monsters.

## Duration Guidelines

| Duration | When Used | Cost Range |
|----------|-----------|------------|
| "until the end of the round" | Most common, buffs/debuffs | 3-5 Malice |
| "until the start of [creature]'s next turn" | Defensive debuffs | 3-5 Malice |
| "(save ends)" or "(EoT)" | Conditions from abilities | Any cost |
| "until the end of the encounter" | RARE - terrain/major effects | 7+ Malice only |

---

## Fog & Mist Theme

> ⭐️ **Veil of Shadow (2 Malice)**
>
> One creature acting this turn shifts up to 5 squares, ignoring difficult terrain and enemies. Until the end of the round, they have concealment.

> 🌀 **Choking Mists (3 Malice)**
>
> Create a 5-cube of mist within 10 squares. The area is heavily obscured until the end of the round. Any creature that starts their turn in the area takes 2 corruption damage.

> 🔳 **Formless Terror (5 Malice)**
>
> | **Area, Magic** | **Main action** |
> | --------------- | --------------: |
> | **📏 3 cube within 5** | **🎯 Each enemy in the area** |
>
> **Power Roll + 2:**
>
> - **≤11:** 5 corruption damage; I < 1 frightened (save ends)
> - **12-16:** 8 corruption damage; I < 2 frightened (save ends)
> - **17+:** 11 corruption damage; I < 3 frightened and slowed (save ends)
>
> **Effect:** The area is heavily obscured until the end of the round.

## Wolf & Beast Theme

> ⭐️ **Pack Tactics (2 Malice)**
>
> Choose one allied beast or wolf within 10 squares. That ally immediately makes a free strike against a creature of your choice within their reach.

> 🌀 **Howl of the Hunt (3 Malice)**
>
> Each enemy within 10 squares makes an **Intuition test**.
>
> - **≤11:** frightened of the howling creature until the end of their next turn
> - **12-16:** slowed until the end of their next turn
> - **17+:** No effect

> ⭐️ **Ravenous Fury (3 Malice)**
>
> One beast acting this turn gains an edge on strikes and deals +3 damage until the end of the round. This effect ends early if the beast takes holy damage.

> 🗡 **Bloody Feast (5 Malice)**
>
> Each beast or wolf in the encounter can make a free strike against a creature adjacent to them. If the strike hits, the beast regains Stamina equal to half the damage dealt.

---

## Undead Theme

> ⭐️ **Grasp of the Grave (2 Malice)**
>
> One creature within 5 squares is restrained until the end of their next turn. They can escape with a Might test as a maneuver.

> 🌀 **Ravenous Horde (2 Malice)**
>
> At the end of this round, each hero not already adjacent to one or more undead is beset by two **rotting zombies** who burst up from the ground to appear in adjacent unoccupied spaces. Each zombie is winded. This feature can't be used two rounds in a row.

> ⭐️ **Life Drain (3 Malice)**
>
> Until the end of the round, the next undead strike that hits allows the undead to regain Stamina equal to half the damage dealt.

> 🔳 **Touch of Death (5 Malice)**
>
> | **Magic, Melee** | **Main action** |
> | ---------------- | --------------: |
> | **📏 Melee 1** | **🎯 One creature** |
>
> **Power Roll + 2:**
>
> - **≤11:** 8 corruption damage; M < 1 weakened (save ends)
> - **12-16:** 12 corruption damage; M < 2 weakened (save ends)
> - **17+:** 15 corruption damage; M < 3 weakened (save ends)
>
> **Effect:** The undead regains Stamina equal to half the damage dealt.

## Witch/Hag Theme

> ⭐️ **Cursed Gift (2 Malice)**
>
> One creature within 10 squares makes a **Reason test**.
>
> - **≤11:** weakened until the end of their next turn
> - **12-16:** dazed until the end of their next turn
> - **17+:** No effect

> 🔳 **Brew of Woe (3 Malice)**
>
> | **Area, Magic** | **Main action** |
> | --------------- | --------------: |
> | **📏 2 cube within 8** | **🎯 Each creature in the area** |
>
> **Power Roll + 2:**
>
> - **≤11:** 4 poison damage; M < 1 dazed (EoT)
> - **12-16:** 6 poison damage; M < 2 dazed (EoT)
> - **17+:** 8 poison damage; M < 3 dazed and weakened (save ends)

> ⭐️ **Binding Words (3 Malice)**
>
> One creature within 10 squares is slowed until the end of their next turn. If they are already slowed, they are restrained until the end of their next turn instead.

> 🌀 **Share Suffering (5 Malice)**
>
> Choose two creatures within 10 squares. Until the end of the round, when one takes damage, the other takes the same amount of corruption damage.

## Cursed Tree/Plant Theme

> ⭐️ **Roots of Binding (2 Malice)**
>
> Each creature in a 3-burst area is slowed until the end of their next turn. They can break free with a Might test as a maneuver.

> 🌀 **Strangling Vines (3 Malice)**
>
> One creature within 5 squares is grabbed and takes 2 damage at the start of each of their turns while grabbed. The grab ends if they succeed on an Agility or Might test.

>

## Dread & Fear Theme

> ⭐️ **Hunter's Edge (3 Malice)**
>
> One creature acting this turn gains an edge on their next strike and deals +3 damage if that strike hits.

> ⭐️ **Stalking Shadow (2 Malice)**
>
> One creature acting this turn becomes hidden until they attack or are discovered. They can shift up to 3 squares while becoming hidden.

> ⭐️ **Aura of Dread (3 Malice)**
>
> Until the end of the round, any enemy that starts their turn within 3 squares of a chosen creature is slowed until the end of their turn.

> ⭐️ **Gaze of Terror (3 Malice)**
>
> One creature within 5 squares that can see the monster is frightened until the end of their next turn. If they are already frightened, they are dazed until the end of their next turn instead.

## Forest Manipulation Theme

> 🔳 **Path of Thorns (3 Malice)**
>
> | **Area** | **Main action** |
> | -------- | --------------: |
> | **📏 5 line within 10** | **🎯 Each enemy in the area** |
>
> **Power Roll + 2:**
>
> - **≤11:** 3 damage; slowed (EoT)
> - **12-16:** 5 damage; A < 1 slowed (save ends)
> - **17+:** 7 damage; A < 2 slowed (save ends)
>
> **Effect:** The area is difficult terrain until the end of the round.

> 🌀 **Trees Attack (5 Malice)**
>
> Each enemy in the encounter takes 4 damage from whipping branches. Additionally, one enemy of your choice is grabbed by a tree (escape with Might test as a maneuver).

> 🌀 **Vines Everywhere (7 Malice)**
>
> Vines rapidly grow across the entire encounter map. Each enemy in the encounter makes an **Agility test**.
>
> - **≤11:** 8 damage; restrained (save ends)
> - **12-16:** restrained (EoT)
> - **17+:** No effect
>
> **Effect:** The encounter map is difficult terrain until the end of the round.

## Solo Monster Features

> ☠️ **Solo Action (5 Malice)**
>
> The monster takes an additional main action on their turn. They can use this feature even if they are dazed.

> 🌀 **Moonfall (10 Malice)**
>
> Until the end of the encounter, the encounter map turns to night and the moon appears impossibly huge in the sky. The monster can take an additional move action or maneuver on each of their turns while they have line of effect to the moon.

## Quick Reference: Cost Guidelines

| Cost | Effect Type | Forest Examples |
|------|-------------|-----------------|
| **2 Malice** | Quick targeted effects, small debuffs | Veil of Shadow, Pack Tactics, Grasp of the Grave |
| **3 Malice** | Moderate control, buffs, small areas | Choking Mists, Howl of the Hunt, Aura of Dread |
| **5 Malice** | Significant abilities, free strikes, Solo Action | Formless Terror, Touch of Death, Whispering Woods |
| **7 Malice** | Map-wide effects, lasting terrain | Vines Everywhere |
| **10 Malice** | Ultimate encounter-changing effects | Moonfall |

---

## Using Malice Features

1. **Choose appropriate cost** - lower costs for quick effects, higher for dramatic moments
2. **Keep durations short** - most effects should last "until the end of the round"
3. **Reserve "until end of encounter"** for 7+ Malice terrain effects only
4. **Match theme to creature** - wolves use Pack Tactics, undead use Grasp of the Grave
5. **Solo monsters always have Solo Action (5 Malice)**
